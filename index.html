Key Factors for Business Fundability

Whether you're looking for $10k, $100k, or even $1 million+, it starts with one question:

Is your business fundable?

1. Consistent Business Name

Ensure your business name is exactly the same across all official documents and online listings.

2. Good Standing Status

Keep your entity active and compliant with your state. File required reports and pay dues regularly.

3. Collateral

Lenders want to see if you have assets to back the loan. Vehicles, equipment, inventory, and real estate can all strengthen your application.

4. Cash Contribution

Show that you’ve personally invested into the business. Lenders want to see your financial commitment before lending their own money.

5. Business Bank Account

Your business banking history contributes to loan readiness. Open and maintain an active account.
