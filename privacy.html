1. Introduction

Rondal Gardner ("we," "us," or "our") operates https://www.businessfunding.help/ (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

At BusinessFunding.Help, we collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. Personal Data

When you interact with our Site, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (when you request the 27-Point Checklist)
  • Business information (if provided in forms)
  • Contact details if you reach out for support

Usage Data

Our systems automatically collect technical information including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on Site
  • Referring website (if applicable)
  • Device characteristics and operating system
  • Click patterns and navigation paths

We utilize cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember your preferences and provide personalized content recommendations while helping us optimize our website's performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to deliver the services you request, such as sending the 27-Point Fundability Checklist to your provided email address. Your contact information enables us to respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and follow up regarding our business funding services. We analyze usage patterns to identify which resources are most valuable to our visitors, allowing us to focus on creating more of that content type.

Specifically, we use collected data to:

  • Deliver the 27-Point Fundability Checklist and promised resources
  • Improve our services and user experience through data analysis
  • Respond to inquiries and provide personalized support
  • Send educational content and funding tips (with opt-out available)
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and enhance website security
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our business interests

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. Limited sharing may occur with:

  • Service Providers: Email platforms (e.g., Formspree) for checklist delivery

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use Google Analytics to understand Site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly. Our cookies help remember your preferences, enable certain functions, and provide analytics to improve our services. We may also use pixel tags and web beacons in our emails to track open rates.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, but no online transmission is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and notify us immediately of any suspected breaches. We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Regular security audits help us identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

7. Your Rights & Choices

As a user of our services, you have important rights regarding your personal information. You may request access to the data we hold about you, including what information we've collected, how we use it, and with whom we share it. If you discover any inaccuracies in your personal data, you have the right to request corrections or updates to ensure our records are current and accurate.

You maintain control over your information through these options:

  • Unsubscribe from emails (via link in each message)

  • Opt out of personalized advertising where available
  • California residents may have additional CCPA rights
