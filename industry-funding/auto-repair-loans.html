5 Costly Mistakes Auto Shops Make

1. Financing equipment through dealers only

While convenient, dealer financing often carries rates 3-5% higher than specialty auto business lenders.

2. Not separating business and personal credit

Establish business credit with tool vendors and parts suppliers to qualify for better terms on larger loans.

3. Underestimating seasonal cash needs

Secure lines of credit during peak seasons (summer/winter) when your financials look strongest.

4. Ignoring equipment certification value

ASE-certified equipment and EV certifications can qualify you for better loan terms and grants.

5. Waiting until emergencies occur

​80% of shop loans are denied when urgently needed - establish financing relationships during stable periods.
