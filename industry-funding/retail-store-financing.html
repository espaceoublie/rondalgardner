  • Unpredictable foot traffic and sales patterns

Top 5 Mistakes Retailers Make When Seeking Financing

1. Relying only on personal credit

Retail businesses need separate business credit profiles (DUNS number, business credit scores) to access better terms and higher limits.

2. Not tracking inventory turns properly

Lenders want to see efficient inventory management (at least 4 turns annually) before extending credit. Use inventory management software to demonstrate this.

3. Ignoring trade credit building

Strong vendor relationships can lead to net-30 and net-60 terms that improve cash flow and demonstrate creditworthiness to other lenders.

4. Applying randomly without preparation

Each credit application creates a hard inquiry. Strategic preparation (financial statements, business plan) increases approval odds.

5. Overlooking local/regional programs

Many municipalities offer retail business grants and low-interest loans that national lenders don't advertise.
