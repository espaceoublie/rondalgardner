Why Retail Businesses Struggle With Financing

The retail industry faces unique hurdles when seeking capital:

  • High inventory costs
  • Fluctuating foot traffic
  • Limited credit history
  • High dependence on consumer trends

Top 3 Mistakes Retailers Make

1. Relying only on personal credit

Retail businesses need separate business credit profiles to access better terms.

2. Not tracking inventory turns

Lenders want to see efficient inventory management before extending credit.

3. Ignoring trade credit building

Strong vendor relationships can lead to better financing options.
